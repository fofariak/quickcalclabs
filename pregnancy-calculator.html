✨ How Our Pregnancy Calculator Works

Our pregnancy calculator is based on Naegele’s rule, which estimates your due date by adding 280 days (40 weeks) to the first day of your last menstrual period (LMP). This provides an easy, reliable way to track your pregnancy progress and know what to expect at each stage.

What You’ll Learn

  • Estimated Due Date (EDD): When your baby is most likely to be born
  • Weeks Pregnant: Track your exact progress week by week
  • Trimester Information: Know which stage of pregnancy you’re in
  • Milestones: Discover your baby’s development at your current week

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this pregnancy calculator?

✔️ It provides a reliable estimate using Naegele’s rule. Your doctor may refine this with an ultrasound.

Can my due date change?

📅 Yes, due dates may be adjusted after ultrasounds if fetal growth suggests a different timeline.

Do most babies arrive on their due date?

👶 Only about 5% of babies are born exactly on their due date. Most arrive within two weeks before or after.
