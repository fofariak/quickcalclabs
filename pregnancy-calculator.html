How Our Pregnancy Calculator Works

Pregnancy calculators use the Naegele’s rule to estimate your due date. It adds 280 days (40 weeks) to the first day of your last menstrual period (LMP). While this is the standard method, remember that only about 5% of babies are born on their exact due date.

What You’ll Learn
