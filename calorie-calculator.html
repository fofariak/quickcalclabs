About the Calorie Calculator

This calculator uses the Mifflin–St Jeor equation to estimate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), then applies your activity level to estimate Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE).

Use it to set goals: eat fewer calories than TDEE for weight loss, or more for muscle gain.
