About QuickCalcLabs

Fast, free, and friendly calculators for health, fitness and everyday life.

Our Mission

QuickCalcLabs was created to provide reliable, easy-to-use calculators that help people make smarter daily decisions — from tracking calories and body metrics to planning important dates. We believe computing should be fast, beautiful, and accessible to everyone.

What We Offer

  • Health & fitness calculators: BMI, Body Fat, Calorie & Macro calculators
  • Women's health tools: Period & Pregnancy calculators with ovulation insights
  • Handy lifestyle tools: Age calculator, Water intake calculator, and more

Quality & Accuracy

We use widely recognized formulas (Mifflin—St Jeor, US Navy method, etc.). Results are estimates — always consult professionals for personalized medical or nutritional advice.

Our commitment to accuracy means we regularly review and update our algorithms based on the latest scientific research and industry standards.
