Introduction

QuickCalcLabs ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when using our free online calculators and tools.

We believe in transparency and want you to understand exactly how your data is handled when you visit our site.

Information we collect

  • Automatically collected data: When you visit our site we collect technical data such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, device type, operating system, and timestamps via server logs and analytics tools.
  • Cookies & similar technologies: We use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to improve site functionality, remember your preferences (like dark mode), and for analytics and advertising purposes.
  • Form submissions: If you contact us via our contact form, we receive the name, email address, subject, and message you submitted voluntarily.
  • Calculator usage: We may collect anonymous data about which calculators are used most frequently to improve our services, but we don't store your actual calculations or results.

How we use your information

  • Service operation: To operate, maintain, and improve our website, calculators, and user experience.
  • Analytics: To analyze usage patterns and trends so we can understand how users interact with our tools and make improvements (primarily through Google Analytics).
  • Monetization: To display contextually relevant advertisements and manage site monetization through services like Google AdSense.
  • Communication: To respond to inquiries, feedback, or support requests you send via our contact forms or email.
  • Technical improvements: To identify and fix bugs, optimize performance, and ensure our calculators work correctly across different devices and browsers.

Advertising & analytics

We use third-party services to understand our audience and serve relevant advertisements:

  • Google Analytics: Helps us measure site traffic, user behavior, and popular content. This service may use cookies and collect anonymized usage data.
  • Google AdSense: Displays contextually relevant advertisements on our site. This service may use cookies to show personalized ads based on your browsing history.
  • Other ad networks: We may partner with other reputable advertising networks that follow similar privacy practices.

These services have their own privacy policies and data handling practices. We encourage you to review Google's privacy policies and opt-out options if you prefer.

Third-party links

Our website may include links to third-party websites, external calculators, or additional resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies separately before providing any personal information.

Data retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and meet legal obligations. Server logs are typically retained for 30-90 days, while contact form submissions may be kept longer to maintain our support history. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (such as GDPR in Europe or CCPA in California), you may have rights including:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we have about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out: Decline certain uses of your information, such as marketing communications.
