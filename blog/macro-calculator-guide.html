Macros Made Simple — How to Calculate Protein, Carbs & Fat

Published: August 26, 2025 • QuickCalcLabs

Macronutrients shape body composition and performance. Our Macro Calculator calculates TDEE, lets you set goal intensity and customize macro ratios with locked sliders and a live chart.

How it works

We compute BMR (Mifflin–St Jeor), multiply by activity to get TDEE, and adjust calories by your goal intensity. The macro sliders split the adjusted calories into protein/carbs/fat, then convert to grams (protein & carbs = 4 kcal/g, fat = 9 kcal/g).

Practical suggestions

  • When cutting, keep protein higher (to preserve muscle).
  • When bulking, raise carbs to fuel training sessions.
  • Use the live chart to visualize splits and hover for grams.
