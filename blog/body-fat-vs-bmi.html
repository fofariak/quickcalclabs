Body Fat vs. BMI: Which Is More Accurate?

Both **Body Fat %** and **BMI** are popular health metrics — but which one gives a true picture of your health? Let’s break it down and show you how to measure both.

Home › Blog › Body Fat vs. BMI

📊 What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a quick formula based on height and weight. It’s widely used by doctors, but it **doesn’t account for muscle mass** or fat distribution.

👉 Try it yourself with our ⚖️ BMI Calculator.

💪 What is Body Fat %?

Body Fat % measures how much of your body weight is fat. This gives a **clearer picture of your health** than BMI alone.

✨ Which One Should You Use?

If you’re an athlete or muscular, BMI may label you “overweight” even if you’re lean. In such cases, **Body Fat % is the better metric**. For general screening, BMI is fine, but always cross-check with Body Fat %.
