Manual method — quick overview

If you want to calculate age yourself: subtract birth date from today's date. Most people break it into years, then months, then days. Beware of leap years — they add an extra day every four years.

Simple example

Born: January 10, 1990 → Today: August 26, 2025

  • Years: 35
  • Months: 7 (January → August)
  • Days: 16
