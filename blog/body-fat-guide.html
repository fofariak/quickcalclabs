Estimate Body Fat at Home — Simple Methods & Calculator

Published: August 26, 2025 • QuickCalcLabs

Body fat percentage is a clearer indicator of body composition than BMI. Our Body Fat Calculator uses the US Navy method — a tape-measure approach that’s practical and quick.

Methods to estimate body fat

  • Tape methods: US Navy formula (neck/waist/hip for women).
  • Skinfold calipers: quick but technique-sensitive.
  • DXA / BodPod: precise clinical tests (costly).

Interpreting results

General healthy ranges: Men (6–24% depending on fitness), Women (14–32%). Consult professionals for targeted goals; use body fat alongside strength and performance metrics.

Measure carefully and consistently — then track progress using the Body Fat Calculator.
