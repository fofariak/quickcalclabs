How Much Water Should You Drink Per Day? Hydration Guide & Calculator

Published: August 26, 2025 • QuickCalcLabs

Hydration needs vary by body size, activity, and climate. The old “8 glasses” rule is a useful baseline but not personalized. Our Water Intake Calculator estimates liters per day using weight, activity and climate adjustments.

How we estimate

We start with roughly 30–35 ml per kg of bodyweight, then increase for higher activity and hot climates. The calculator gives liters, ounces, and cups to make the guidance practical.

Tips for staying hydrated

  • Carry a bottle and sip regularly.
  • Drink before, during and after exercise.
  • Use water-rich foods (fruit, soups) to supplement intake.
