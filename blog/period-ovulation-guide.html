Tracking cycles improves reproductive health awareness, helps plan for pregnancy, and aids symptom management. Our Period & Ovulation Calculator predicts upcoming periods and fertile windows based on your cycle length and last period.

How ovulation and fertility work

Ovulation — the release of an egg from the ovary — typically occurs about 14 days before your next period. Fertility is highest in the 5 days leading up to and the day of ovulation. Cycle length varies; tracking helps predict your unique pattern.

Use the calculator

Enter your last period date and average cycle length into the Period Calculator to receive predicted period dates, ovulation day, and fertile window ranges.

Limitations

Stress, illness, travel, and hormonal changes can shift cycle timing. The tool provides estimates — consult a healthcare professional for fertility planning or cycle irregularities.

Quick tips

  • Track at least 3–6 cycles to improve prediction accuracy.
  • Note symptoms (cervical mucus, basal body temp) for fertility clues.
  • Use the calculator regularly and update cycle length as it changes.
