Disclosure & How We Support Our Site

Last updated: September 30, 2025

Our Commitment to Transparency

At QuickCalcLabs, our primary goal is to provide free, accurate, and easy-to-use calculators. To keep our tools and content free, we generate revenue through affiliate marketing and programmatic advertising. Maintaining your trust is paramount, so we believe in being 100% transparent about how we operate.

This page explains how we make money and our strict commitment to editorial integrity.

How We Fund QuickCalcLabs

We use two primary methods to cover the costs of running this site, including server hosting, development, and content creation:

1. Affiliate Links

Some of the links on our website are "affiliate links." This means that if you click on one of these links and make a purchase from the vendor, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you.

Our Promise: We only recommend products and services that we believe will genuinely provide value to our users. Our recommendations are based on our own research and expertise. Earning a commission is a secondary consideration and never influences our decision to feature a product.

2. Programmatic Advertising

We display ads on our pages, which are served through advertising networks like Google AdSense. These ads help us cover our operational costs and keep our calculators free for everyone.

We strive to ensure the ads displayed are relevant and unobtrusive, but we do not have direct control over every individual ad that is shown.

Our Editorial Integrity is Our Priority

Your trust is more important than any commission or ad revenue. We want to be very clear on this point:

  • The formulas and results of our calculators are based on scientific evidence and are never influenced by our affiliate partnerships or advertisers.
  • Our informational content and blog posts are written to be accurate, objective, and helpful. We do not accept payment for positive reviews.
  • All sponsored content, if any, will be clearly and conspicuously marked as such.

Our mission is to provide you with the best tools possible, and we will never compromise on our integrity to make a profit.
