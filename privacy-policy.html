Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 27, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to QuickCalcLabs ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, quickcalclabs.com (the "Site"). By using the Site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Our guiding principle is to collect only the minimum information necessary. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use our calculators.

  • Personal Data: The only personal data we collect is what you voluntarily provide to us when you contact us via email, such as your name and email address.
  • Calculator Data: Any data you enter into our calculators (e.g., age, weight, height) is processed in your browser and is not stored on our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To operate and maintain our Site.
  • To improve the user experience and develop new features.
  • To understand and analyze how you use our Site through analytics.
  • To respond to your comments, questions, and provide customer service.
  • To serve advertisements that help keep our services free.

4. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like many other websites, we use 'cookies'. These are small files stored on your device that help the Site function and allow us to analyze traffic. We use cookies to understand user preferences for future visits and compile aggregate data about site traffic and interaction.

You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options. However, this may affect your ability to use certain features of our Site.

5. Third-Party Services & Advertising

We use third-party services to help us operate our Site:

  • Google AdSense: We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our Site. Google uses cookies (such as the DoubleClick DART cookie) to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website and other websites on the Internet. Users may opt-out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network privacy policy.
  • Google Analytics: We use this service to understand our website traffic and user engagement. Google Analytics may collect your IP address, but we do not combine this information with any personally identifiable information.

These third-party services have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
